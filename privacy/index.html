✈︎ Trippie Policies

Privacy Policy

Updated: December 2025

Trippie helps you plan and share trips with friends. We collect only the information needed to provide the service and we never sell your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as your name and email address.
  • Trip information you create or import in the app.
  • Diagnostics that help us improve performance and reliability.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Trippie account.
  • To sync trip content across your devices and invitees.
  • To send important updates about service changes or issues.

Your Choices

  • Update or delete your account data at any time from the app.
  • Contact us to request a copy of your data or to deactivate your account.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications from email footers.
